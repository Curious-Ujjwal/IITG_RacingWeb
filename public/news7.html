30-01-2017

Team Evolution to participate in FB 2017.

IITG Racing participated in Formula Bharat 2017 which was organized in Coimbatore from 25-29 January. Our team reached the venue much before the event and scored well in the events we can participate. We ranked 10th in Business event, 15th in the Cost event & 28th rank in the Design event. We couldn’t compete in Dynamic events and achieved 21st rank in the overall competition.

The event was won by VIT Vellore’s team with 747.92 points. Our team scored a total of 150.72 points.
