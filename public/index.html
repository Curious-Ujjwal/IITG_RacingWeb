A NEW BEGINNNING

The Automobile Club of IIT Guwahati comprises a group of hardworking individuals who share a common passion - automobiles. Our love for the latest technologies and innovations in the realm of automobiles drives us to endeavour to design and fabricate vehicles of our own. We take part in several competitions like SUPRA SAE, SAE Efficycle. Our love for automobiles also drives us to spread this knowledge and help ignite this passion in others. We organise lectures on Automobiles and their various sub-systems. We also hold workshops to teach softwares such as Solidworks.
